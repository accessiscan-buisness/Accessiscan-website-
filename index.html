Frequently Asked Questions

What is ADA compliance for websites?

The Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) requires businesses to provide equal access to individuals with disabilities. For websites, this means ensuring that people with visual, auditory, cognitive, and motor impairments can use and navigate your site effectively.
